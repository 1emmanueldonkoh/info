Bill Gates Vrs Interviewer

In an Interview with Bill Gates, the richest man in the world, the interviewer asked him,
"Sir, what is the secret behind your success?" He gave her a cheque from his chequebook
and told her to write down the amount she wanted.
She said,"I do not mean this sir," and then She repeated the same question in another way.
He gave her the same answer as he handed her the cheque she had refused to collect but she
still declined. He tore the cheque and then said to her......
"The secret of my success is that I don't miss opportunities as you did now.
You could have been the richest broadcaster in the world".

LESSON TO LEARN
Opportunities won't always be there, seize them when they come your way.
